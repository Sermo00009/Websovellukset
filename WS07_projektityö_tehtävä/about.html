About This Site

What is Dota 2?

Dota 2 is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed by Valve. Players compete in two teams of five, picking from over 100 unique heroes. The goal: destroy the enemy's Ancient base before they destroy yours.

Why this site?

This website is made by a passionate fan to help beginners learn about the heroes, mechanics, and beauty of the game. You’ll find hero tips, lore, and visuals here — all in one place.
